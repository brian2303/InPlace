Welcome to InPlace

Colombian company with more than 30 years of experience in the manufacture and commercialization of articles of use in the home and industry, time in which one has worked actively to obtain a strong and lasting opportunity of businesses. In our organization we are continuously innovating and creating ideas that allow us to improve processes and maximize our level and quality of production. The objective of our company is to develop products for man and future life that satisfy the needs of demanding markets. Our business focus is to develop products and services, based on absolute quality and excellent customer service, which ensure a substantial and tangible impact on productivity and competitiveness, so our target group is the business, industrial, commercial and domestic sector. It is important to emphasize that our great strength is based on three important points which are:

* Customer Focus
* Innovation
* High Performance

Added to this, the excellent human group highly qualified in the development of each one of the processes carried out, and the great experience that throughout these years we have acquired, with the only objective to offer the client the best of our company.
